About Me

My name is Keller and I am the founder of Distant Lands. My journey into entrepreneurship began in 2018, fueled by a lifelong love for video game development. I've been immersed in creating games since I was a kid, and that passion led me to specialize in Unity development.

Distant Lands isn't just about crafting captivating games; it's also about sharing my expertise and supporting budding developers. I've always had a fervor for helping newcomers in the field. My goal is to provide the necessary tools and resources to kickstart their projects.

For me, Distant Lands is more than a business; it's a commitment to nurturing a community of aspiring developers. I aim to foster a space where innovation thrives, and individuals are empowered to create and collaborate. My vision is to inspire others and create a platform where creativity flourishes alongside a shared enthusiasm for game development.
